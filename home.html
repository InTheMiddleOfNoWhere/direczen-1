Direczen is a full service travel provider that specializes in offering nonprofits an affordable way to find local accommodations in real time. We were created by two former first reponders to Hurricane Katrina, when we realized how complex and expensive it is to mobilize humanitarian efforts. We use our 501(c)(3) status to get competitive vendor rates, expecially for those doing good work!
